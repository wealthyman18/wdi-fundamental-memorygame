Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suuijaku,Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cords are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over of matching cards.
